# Gemini Direct Vision Analysis

## Overview
Forensic analysis of the Wright edition facsimile images to confirm the presence of historical characters (long-s) and ligatures.

## Page 11 (leaf B2 verso / B3 recto) [011_b2_verso_b3_recto.jpg]
### Sonnet 10 (Recto)
- **Long-s count**: 9 detected in first 4 lines
- **Sample text**:
  - line 1: `For ſhame deny that thou bear'ſt loue to any,`
  - line 2: `Who for thy ſelfe art ſo vnprouident`
  - line 3: `Graunt if thou wilt, thou art belou'd of many,`
  - line 4: `But that thou none lou'ſt is moſt euident:`
- **Ligatures detected**: `ſt` (bear'ſt), `ſt` (lou'ſt).
